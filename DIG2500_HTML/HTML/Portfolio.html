About

This is the portfolio I submitted to the UCF Graphic Design program. The program is very selective, only accepting 70 students per year, when around 300 apply. It was very nerve wracking, because you can only submit once, and if I wasn't accepted, I have no clue what to study.

The portfolio requirements:

  • Submitted as a PDF, 1920x1080
  • Title page and a statement of interest
  • Five personal works
  • Design a brand based on one of their prompts
